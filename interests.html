Moda

Moda is my best buddy, he is a playful "cow cat" (black-and-white cat), and he always looks like he's wearing a tuxedo. He is two years old and was adopted in California. In early 2026, he joined me on a cross-country road trip across the United States; we set out from California, traveling through Arizona, New Mexico, Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi, and Alabama before finally arriving in Georgia. He remained by my side throughout my journies, right up until I successfully defended my doctoral dissertation.
